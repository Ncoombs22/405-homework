Directions

  1. 1/2 (16 ounce) package linguine
    • 1 cup fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into cubes.
    • 1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or 25% Less Sodium)
      • 1/2 cup milk 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Prepare the filling.
    • Cook linguine according to package directions. Add broccoli for last 4 minutes of cooking time. Drain.
    • Heat butter in skillet. Cook chicken until browned, stirring often.
    • Add soup, milk, cheese, black pepper and linguine mixture and heat through. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese.
    • preheat the oven for 15 minutes at 350 degress.
    • after oven is preheated than put serving in for 25 minutes
  4. If you want you can always add more butter and cheese to it to get more flavor.
    • After the meal is done you let it cool off for about 5 minutes.
    • The meal should be ready to be served.
    • Enjoy your meal
